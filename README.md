#Tonikaku Kawaii (トニカクカワイイ)

## Overview

**Tonikaku Kawaii** (also known as *Fly Me to the Moon*) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by **Kenjiro Hata**. It is a romantic comedy that follows the story of Nasa Yuzaki and Tsukasa Tsukuyomi after a fateful encounter on a snowy night.

## Synopsis

Nasa Yuzaki, a boy with an unfortunate name, meets a mysterious girl named Tsukasa Tsukuyomi on a snowy night after a near-fatal accident. Despite barely knowing each other, Tsukasa agrees to go out with Nasa — but only if they get married first. What follows is a heartwarming and comedic journey of their married life, filled with love, mystery, and a hint of the supernatural surrounding Tsukasa's true identity.

## Why Read This Manga?

- **Pure Romance:** A refreshing take on married life in manga, focusing on a committed relationship rather than the usual "will-they-won't-they" trope.
- **Wholesome Vibes:** Extremely heartwarming and feel-good moments throughout.
- **Mystery Element:** Gradually reveals the truth behind Tsukasa's immortality and connection to the moon.
- **Great Comedy:** Balances romance with genuinely funny situations and character interactions.
